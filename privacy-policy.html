Summary: We collect minimal data needed to run our site. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. You have the right to access, correct or delete your data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

The World Cup Times ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at this domain. We collect information in the following ways:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Newsletter subscriptions: When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.
  • Contact forms: If you contact us, we collect your name, email address and the content of your message.
  • Comments & ratings: If you submit article ratings or comments, we collect that content along with a timestamp.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages viewed, time spent, links clicked and referring URLs.
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution and IP address (anonymised after 24 hours).
  • Cookies: See Section 3 for full details on our use of cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and improve our website content, match reviews and analysis.
  • Send newsletters and match-day updates to subscribers (you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • Respond to enquiries and support requests.
  • Analyse site traffic and user behaviour to improve the reader experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity.

We will never use your information for purposes materially different from those described above without first seeking your consent.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our site. Our cookies fall into four categories:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our content (e.g., Google Analytics — anonymised).
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings, such as cookie consent choices.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising (only where you have consented).

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our Cookie Settings page.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate the site (email delivery, analytics, hosting) under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: Where required by law, court order or government authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the successor entity.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy:

  • Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Contact form submissions are retained for 12 months.
  • Anonymised analytics data is retained for up to 26 months.
  • Cookie preference records are retained for 12 months.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion: Request that your data be erased ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for direct marketing purposes.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent for processing at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details in Section 10.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites, including social media platforms and other football media outlets. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify subscribers by email. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact our Privacy Officer:

  • Email: privacy@worldcuptimes.com
  • Post: The World Cup Times, Privacy Officer, 1 Stadium Plaza, New York, NY 10001, USA
  • Response time: We will respond to all enquiries within 30 days.

Have a Privacy Question?

Our Privacy Officer is available to answer any questions or concerns about how we handle your data.
